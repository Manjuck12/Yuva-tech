Welcome to GS Groups

The Geetha Shiva Foundation, a journey that commenced in 1996 with the establishment of the New Education Public School in Yelahanka, Bangalore.
Since its inception, the GS Groups have been unwavering in their commitment to providing excellence in education. With over 1800 students enrolled in various institutions, offering programs in distance education, arts and science, across two vibrant cities, Bangalore and Davangere, our belief is simple: quality education should be affordable and accessible to all.
